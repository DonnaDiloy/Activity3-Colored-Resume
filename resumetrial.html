ABOUT ME


Licensed Massage Therapist | Helping people who are needs of some relaxation, or relief of muscle pain, tightness, or tension.


I'm graduate of The Bestech (The Bethel Educational Support and Technology). I like to incorporate my light to medium pressure relaxation massage in with myofascial release and trigger point therapy. Combining these different techniques makes for a relaxing, yet effective massage in relieving tight muscles from everyday use or injuries from overuse. I also specializes in Hot Stone Therapy and Thai Combination technique. My treatments offer a light therapeutic touch, for those who would like to avoid a deep pressure treatment. Medium to deep pressure for those who would like reach deep fascia.


I decided to become a massage therapist because I love helping people. At the end of the day, I finds such fulfillment knowing I have made a difference in helping my clients feel better. My plans are to expand my knowledge of energy healing and to help attune myself to understand client's injuries even better. I strongly believe in healing from within to properly maintain the body.
